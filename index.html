FAQ

Questions, answered.

What exactly is FundVisory?+

FundVisory is an Indian equity opportunity discovery platform. It continuously scans the NSE, ranks every name by a 0–100 opportunity score, and surfaces the few stocks setting up for a move — each with a clear entry, stop, target and the reasoning behind it.

Is this a tip service or a buy/sell signal provider?+

No. FundVisory is research & analysis software. It helps you find and study opportunities and shows illustrative, paper-traded outcomes — it does not place orders, manage money, or provide personalised investment advice. Always do your own research and consult a SEBI-registered adviser.

How is the opportunity score calculated?+

Each stock is scored 0–100 from a blend of trend stage, relative strength versus the NIFTY, sector leadership, volume behaviour and breakout readiness. The stronger and cleaner the setup, the higher the score, so the best opportunities rise to the top of the board.

What happened to the Fair Value Analyzer?+

It's still here — now as a secondary module. FundVisory's primary job is discovering opportunities; the Fair Value Analyzer (DCF, Graham, Dhandho) is the long-term valuation lens you reach for once you've found a name worth studying.

How do I get access during the private beta?+

Request access using the form above. Every application is reviewed by hand. If you're approved, you'll receive an email invite to set up your password, after which you can sign in at app.fundvisory.in.

Which market does it cover?+

FundVisory is built for Indian equities and scans 1,033+ NSE-listed stocks daily. It's designed specifically around the NSE — sectors, regime and relative strength are all measured against the Indian market.
